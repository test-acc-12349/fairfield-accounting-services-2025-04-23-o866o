Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our accounting services

We combine cutting-edge technology with personalized service. Each client gets a dedicated account manager who truly understands their business, plus access to our team of specialists for complex needs. Our proactive approach helps identify opportunities and risks before they become problems.

We offer transparent, tiered pricing based on your business size and needs. Most clients choose our monthly subscription model which includes all core services, with add-ons available for specialized needs. During your free consultation, we'll provide a customized quote with no obligation.

While we serve businesses across all sectors, we have particular expertise in technology startups, professional services, retail/e-commerce, and hospitality. Our team includes specialists familiar with the unique accounting requirements of these industries.

We use bank-grade encryption for all data transfers and storage. Our systems undergo regular security audits, and we maintain strict access controls. All team members receive comprehensive security training and background checks. We're happy to sign NDAs and can accommodate most specific security requirements.

Our onboarding is designed to be thorough yet efficient. After your initial consultation, we'll gather necessary documents and access (via secure methods). Within 2 weeks, we'll have your accounts set up, historical data analyzed, and provide you with your first financial health report. The entire process is guided by your dedicated account manager.
